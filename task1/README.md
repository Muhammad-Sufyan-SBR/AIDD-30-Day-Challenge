1. What does AI-Driven Development (AIDD) mean to you?

AI-Driven Development yani software development ka ek naya dor jahan AI tools developer ki madad se coding aur software banane ke process ko bohat zyada tezi aur behtari se perform karta hai. Yeh approach coding ko sirf syntax likhne se aage le jaata hai aur developer ko ek intelligent collaborator banata hai jo AI ke saath mil kar design, reasoning, aur testing karta hai. 2025 ke October ka AI Turning Point AI ko mainstream karne wala event tha, jahan AI tools jaise ChatGPT, Gemini, aur Claude development me har developer ki productivity 5 se 10 guna tak barha diye.

2. How do you see the future of Human-AI Collaboration in software development?

Mera khayal hai ke future me software development me AI aur humans ke darmiyan collaboration ziada gahra hoga. Developers AI ko simple coding ke liye nahi balki ek intelligent partner ki tarah use karenge jo complex problems solve karne me, testing me, aur system design me madad karega. Humans AI ko direction denge aur AI unka kaam speed aur accuracy ke saath pura karega. Is se developer ka role badal kar ek system architect aur thinker ban jayega jo AI agents ko control karta hai.

3. What made October 2025 the global AI Turning Point?

October 2025 me AI tools ka development itna advanced aur accessible ho gaya ke AI-assisted development sab developers ke liye aam ho gaya. Gemini CLI, ChatGPT, Claude jaise powerful AI agents ne repetitive tasks automate kar diye aur developer productivity 5 se 10 times badha di. Is milestone ne software development ko AI-powered era me enter karaya, jahan AI developers ka integral partner ban gaya.


---

4. Define the following key concepts briefly:

AI Turning Point (Oct 2025): Woh waqt jab AI tools ne software development me mainstream adoption hasil ki, aur AI developers ke rozmarra ke tools ban gaye.

Agentic AI: AI systems jo independently sochte, plan karte, aur kaam karte hain bina manual intervention ke.

Evaluation-Driven Development (EvDD): Development approach jisme AI-generated code ko evaluate aur improve kiya jata hai, taake high quality aur correctness mile.

Test-Driven Development (TDD): Classic software testing method jisme tests pehle likhe jate hain aur phir code likha jata hai, jo AI workflows me bhi bohat useful hai.

AI Productivity Boom: AI ki madad se developer ki productivity 5-10 guna barhna coding aur testing dono me.
